Crazy Patterns/Di Matto

There is some collection of crazy patterns in the literature, and some crazy exercises in one or more moves. The diagrams presented here show first the threat (THAT IS IMPORTANT TO HAVE IMPRESSED IN THE MIND), and then the execution. The attacking piece is positioned far away, because it is more difficult to see.

Matto di corridoio. The rook supported by knight or bishop, which threatens Tf1 #

Matto di Torre e Cavallo. The King in a corner (Arab Mate); not Txc2, but Th2 #

Matto di corridoio da Donna sola. From many origins, with De1 #. With De8 it is evident, as with Dh4, while with D in a5 and the chessboard full of other pieces, it is not so visible. It also works with two or a pawn, with the appropriate arrangement (third diagram).

These diagrams are a little more useful than simply displaying the final position of a fool, but they are not enough. We must not reason, but recognize them almost instantly. And not only on the empty board, but drowned in the game, as in the diagram below, a real game, where the many pieces around distract the attention from the madman. In reality, if you have eyes to look, not only see the minor threat of Dxe6 + and your chance to earn a tower, the bait. The game continued with Dh1 +? (Dh5), Re3, Dxa1? , De8 #

Crazy with 2 towers on the 7th side. Possible if the King has only 2 escape houses, as below, not possible if he has 3, as above (apart from losing 3 pedestrians). Tg2 +, Rh1, Th2 +, Rg1, Tc g1 #

Crazy where he was pushed the pedestrian in g3 (or equivalent) with Queen + Pawn or Bishop, even far. Never fallen?

The difficult fool of Horse and Bishop, good to do it in 50 moves. King and bishop leave only two houses free for the king. The horse pushes him into a corner, and the bishop gives it crazy. Clockwise. Cc6 +, Ra8, Ab7 #

Horse drowned mad, clockwise, Queen's sacrifice, and rare with a single piece. Db8 +, Torre catches, horse gives crazy.

Assumes total absence of pawns.

Theoretical Draw

The defender must stay under the pawn or in opposition to the King. The attacker must be able to occupy the critical spots, one of the three spots in front of the pawn with two spaces away. After he drives the king.

Con Torre contro Alfiere è patta se il Re è nell'angolo di colore contrario al suo alfiere, perchè questo può bloccare lo scacco in una casa adiacente al Re. In questo caso, mossa al nero, Ad4, Te1+, Ag1.
